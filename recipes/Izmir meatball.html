Izmir meatball

Description

Izmir meatball is an Turkished based dish. Originally it includes meat potato and tomato.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato paste
  • Potato
  • Green fresh pepper
  • Egg
  • Oil
  • Salt and spices

Steps

  1. Mix egg meat and make little balls
  2. Cut potato in shape you like
  3. Make tomato mixture
  4. Cut peppers
  5. Bake in the oven
  6. Let the dish rest 5 min
