Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is an Italian based dish. Originally it includes meat and tomato.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato paste
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheese
  • Egg
  • Oil
  • Salt and spices

Steps

  1. Cook meat and meat sauce
  2. Cook lasagna noodles
  3. Make ricotta mixture
  4. Layer lasagna according to recipe instruction
  5. Bake in the oven
  6. Let the lasagna rest 5 min
